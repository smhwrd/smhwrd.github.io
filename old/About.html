About

Simon grew up in Belfast, Northern Ireland, where he studied at the Sonic Arts Research Centre and was awarded a Bachelors degree in Music Technology. Here he discovered creative programming and interactive sound, and started developing real-time responsive systems for solo performance. He moved to Scotland in 2018, completing a Masters in Sound Design at the University of Edinburgh. He has since been involved in sound production for various films, animations and multimedia exhibitions. Now based in Glasgow, he works as a freelance sound designer, develops short indie games and produces electronic music.
